About ChefCode

We're revolutionizing kitchen management through innovative AI technology, empowering chefs and restaurant owners to achieve unprecedented efficiency and profitability.

Our Story

ChefCode was born from a simple observation: while restaurants embraced technology in the front of house, kitchen operations remained stuck in the past. Chefs were still juggling paper tickets, manual inventory counts, and complex spreadsheets while trying to create culinary excellence.

Our founders, a team of chefs and AI engineers, recognized this gap and set out to create a solution that would honor the artistry of cooking while harnessing the power of artificial intelligence. We believed that technology should enhance, not replace, the human touch in the kitchen.

Today, ChefCode serves thousands of restaurants worldwide, from small bistros to large hotel chains, helping them reduce waste, save time, and boost profits while maintaining the passion and creativity that makes great food truly special.

Our Journey

From concept to global leader in kitchen AI

The Beginning

2021

Founded by a team of chefs and AI engineers who recognized the need for intelligent kitchen management. Initial prototype developed in partnership with three San Francisco restaurants.

First Product Launch

2022

Launched ChefCode v1.0 with voice commands, inventory management, and basic analytics. Achieved 95% customer satisfaction in pilot program with 50 restaurants.

Rapid Growth

2023

Expanded to 500+ restaurants across the US. Introduced predictive analytics, HACCP compliance, and multi-location management. Raised Series A funding.

Global Expansion

2024

Launched internationally with support for multiple languages and currencies. Now serving 2,000+ restaurants worldwide with advanced AI capabilities.
